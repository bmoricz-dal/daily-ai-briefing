### 36. Leading in the Digital Age: Digital Leadership Capabilities, Organizational Innovation Climate, and AI Adoption Intention Among SMEs in Nigeria
- Source: Crossref AI adoption SMEs
- Type: academic_metadata
- Relevance score: 5/5
- Published: 2026
- URL: https://doi.org/10.20944/preprints202605.1631.v1
- DOI: 10.20944/preprints202605.1631.v1
- Authors: Ayodeji Idowu, Yemisi Tomilola Babalola
- Summary: Although small and medium enterprises (SMEs) anchor employment and output across Sub-Saharan Africa, their uptake of artificial intelligence (AI) lags global benchmarks, and prevailing explanations dwell on capital, infrastructure, and institutional voids while overlooking the leadership competencies that determine whether available resources are mobilised at all.
